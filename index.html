Our Story

A Kitchen Born from
Love & Community

La Cocina de Consuelo — Consuelo's Kitchen — began as something as simple and beautiful as feeding people who needed nourishment. In the 1980s, Connie Rodriguez started cooking for a local church fundraiser, and her enchiladas became the talk of the congregation.

What started in a church hall grew decade by decade into a beloved Austin institution. Today, Letty Garcia carries that same spirit: hand-crafting tortillas each morning, mixing authentic sauces from memory, and welcoming every guest like family.

We're proud to serve authentic Mexican cuisine in the heart of Central Austin — with BYOB, daily lunch specials, full catering services, and a menu that mixes tradition with Connie's original flair for new flavors.
